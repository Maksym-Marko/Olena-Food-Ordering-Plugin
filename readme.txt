Olena Food Ordering - a simple yet powerful WordPress plugin for restaurants and food trucks. Easily manage your menu, handle orders with custom add-ons, and organize pickups. Mobile-friendly interface ensures smooth ordering experience for your customers

== Description ==

Transform your restaurant or food truck's online presence with Olena Food Ordering WordPress plugin designed for seamless food ordering. Enable customers to browse your menu, customize orders with add-ons, and schedule convenient pickups. Perfect for small restaurants and food trucks looking to boost their digital ordering capabilities without complexity.

Key features:
- Easy menu management with
- Smart add-on system for food customization
- Mobile-friendly ordering interface
- Demo import of dishes and add-ons
- Currency symbol and other settings in the admin panel

Streamline your food service operations and enhance customer experience with this lightweight, user-friendly plugin.

== Installation ==

= From your WordPress dashboard =

1. Visit 'Plugins > Add New'.
2. Search for 'Olena Food Ordering'.
3. Activate the plugin from your Plugins page.
4. Make a quick demo import of dishes and add-ons.

= From WordPress.org =

1. Download 'Olena Food Ordering'.
2. Upload the 'Olena Food Ordering' directory to your '/wp-content/plugins/' directory, using your favorite method (ftp, sftp, scp, etc...)
3. Activate 'Olena Food Ordering' from your Plugins page.
4. Make a quick demo import of dishes and add-ons.
